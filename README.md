# 🚀 Agentic Revenue Intelligence & Supply Chain Optimization System

## 🎯 The Business Impact
Most analytics portfolios stop at descriptive reporting—building dashboards that simply tell managers what already happened. I built an **Agentic AI system** to prescribe exactly how to fix the problem and quantify the exact ROI of the solution.

By autonomously querying a semantic model of over 100,000 e-commerce orders, this system uncovered a massive, hidden revenue leak: **late deliveries were permanently destroying customer lifetime value.** Instead of spending 15+ hours manually slicing data to find the root cause, I deployed a localized AI Agent via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). The agent identified the logistics bottleneck, proved the exact drop in customer sentiment, and generated a prescriptive simulator to model the financial recovery.

## 💡 Key Business Discoveries
* **The Logistics Bottleneck**: Isolated **$1.35M** in lifetime revenue actively at risk due to late deliveries. The **SP (São Paulo)** region was identified as the primary operational bottleneck, bleeding **$380,000** alone.
* **Customer Sentiment Impact**: Discovered a direct correlation between freight delays and churn. Late deliveries caused a massive drop in average review scores (crashing from **4.3** down to **2.5**), instantly converting loyal customers into detractors.
* **Prescriptive ROI Simulator**: Engineered an interactive What-If parameter proving that a 99% improvement in supply chain SLAs will directly recover **$1.34M** in lost revenue.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
* **Data Engineering**: PostgreSQL (ETL pipeline, view creation, and schema flattening).
* **Semantic Modeling**: Power BI (Star Schema, Time-Intelligence DAX, What-If Parameters).
* **AI Orchestration**: Model Context Protocol (MCP) for autonomous schema auditing and insight generation and for autonomous root-cause analysis and DAX generation.
